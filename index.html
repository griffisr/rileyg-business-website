Par 3 Course & Range

Welcome to Mighty Oak Golf Course, a charming, family-run par 3, 9-hole gem nestled in Brooklyn, Michigan. Formerly known as Silver Lake Golf Course, our course offers a welcoming atmosphere with beautifully maintained fairways and mature trees. Whether you're perfecting your swing at our driving range or enjoying a leisurely round with family and friends, Mighty Oak provides a delightful golfing experience for players of all skill levels. Join us and discover the joy of golf in a serene and picturesque setting.
